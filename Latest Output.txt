Headline findings (all HAC-5, placebo-verified where applicable)
BTC volatility fell 22% post-ETF (3.18% → 2.49% daily SD). SPY correlation edged from 0.32 → 0.37, VIX anti-correlation from –0.32 → –0.35.
ETF flow is the single tightest post-2024 driver of BTC daily returns: btc_etf_intensity β=2.33, HAC t=8.7, bumping R² from ~14% to 23.7% (n=821). Survives no-winsor (R2), HAC 5/10/20/40 (R3), and stays identical with 1-day lag added (R1 reveals next-day mean reversion of ~1/3 of the same-day move — i.e., not naïvely causal).
Chow test at 2024-01-11 does NOT reject stable loadings (F=0.81, p=0.60). The sup-F sweep places the dominant structural break at 2021-01-04 (BTC) / 2021-05-12 (ETH), placebo p=0.00. The 2024 ETFs changed composition (ETH R² 15.4% → 21.0%), not the single-break profile.
Diebold-Yilmaz total connectedness = 27.3% over the 8-variable VAR(1). ETH: 67% of variance from BTC shocks; DeFi TVL: 46% from BTC; BTC itself: 99.5% own.
Event studies: BTC ETF [-1,+1] CAR −8.2% (placebo p=0.61 — not out of distribution); ETH ETF [0,+30] CAR −29.3% (placebo p=0.92); FTX collapse [-1,+1] CAR −17.7% (t=−3.67); SVB [0,+5] CAR +16.8% (t=+3.10) — the only clean two-digit-significance episode in the set.
