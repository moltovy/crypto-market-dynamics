# 004: Private Process Artifacts

## Decision

Keep manager notes, release-process worklogs, private review notes, and
historical packet debris outside the public repository.

## Rationale

Those artifacts can be useful during release management, but they are not part
of the research evidence, reproducibility contract, or public reader workflow.

## Consequence

The public surface presents Crypto Market Dynamics as one clean project while
durable architecture decisions remain in `docs/decisions/`.
