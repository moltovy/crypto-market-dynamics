# Interpretation Guardrails

- ETF-flow results are reduced-form association, exposure, lead-lag, and
  market-plumbing diagnostics. They do not prove ETF flows caused BTC or ETH
  returns.
- Stablecoin supply and TVL are liquidity proxies, not identified liquidity
  shocks.
- Structural-break diagnostics are Chow tests and single-break sup-F scans, not
  full Bai-Perron multiple-break estimation.
- Block partial R2 and exact Shapley R2 depend on block definitions and selected
  features.
- Daily data cannot identify intraday mechanisms.
- Frozen data improves reproducibility but is not a live market monitor.
