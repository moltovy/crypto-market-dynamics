# Crypto Research Summary

Crypto Market Factor Lab focuses on BTC/ETH market structure from 2020 through
April 2026. It covers ETF flows, stablecoin supply, DeFi TVL, macro risk,
TradFi co-movement, and BTC-native valuation/flow variables.

The ETF-flow module scales daily flows by prior-day market cap and studies
lead-lag association. The stablecoin module treats stablecoin supply as a
liquidity proxy. The BTC-native lab separates MVRV valuation state from
exchange/miner flow-state variables.
