# Quant Research Summary

This project demonstrates factor modeling and reduced-form econometrics across
BTC/ETH returns. The v2.1 release includes HAC OLS, block partial R2, nested
ablations, ETF lead-lag regressions, rolling correlations, structural-break
diagnostics, VAR/FEVD connectedness, and event studies.

The main research discipline is interpretive restraint: results are framed as
association, exposure, contribution, and diagnostics rather than causal claims.
