# Limitations

- Daily data limits sequencing and can hide intraday causality.
- ETF-flow results are association and market plumbing, not causal estimates.
- Stablecoin supply and TVL are proxies, not identified liquidity shocks.
- MVRV can mechanically co-move with BTC returns.
- Current structural breaks are Chow and single-break sup-F, not full
  Bai-Perron.
- Portfolio v2.1 block partial R2 is not Shapley/Owen.
- Frozen data is excellent for reproducibility but not a live monitoring system.
