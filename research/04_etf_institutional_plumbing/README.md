# 04_etf_institutional_plumbing: ETF and Institutional Plumbing

## Research Question

How do ETF flow intensity and absorption measures line up with contemporaneous and lagged crypto outcomes?

## Current Findings

- BTC ETF flow-intensity return correlation is 0.379 at lag 0 versus 0.049 at lag 1.
- ETH ETF flow-intensity return correlation is 0.226 at lag 0 versus 0.086 at lag 1.
