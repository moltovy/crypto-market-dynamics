# 02_macro_cross_asset_exposure: Macro and Cross-Asset Exposure

## Research Question

How does crypto co-movement with equities, volatility, rates, dollar, and gold vary by asset, calendar, and period?

## Current Findings

- BTC daily equity-block delta R2 moved from 0.0249 pre-BTC-ETF to 0.0884 in the BTC-ETF era.
- ETH daily equity-block delta R2 moved from 0.0193 pre-BTC-ETF to 0.1076 in the BTC-ETF era.
